meet the owners

Sonia walked over to the window and reflected on her natural surroundings. She had always loved transcendent nature, watching sunlight bounce off the beach. It was a place that encouraged her. Sonia gulped. She glanced at her own reflection. She was a trustworthy, spirited, green tea drinker with feathery feet and blonde hair. Once, she had even brought a defiant old swan back from the brink of death. But not even a trustworthy person who had once brought a defiant old swan back from the brink of death, was prepared for what Andrea had in store today. She said, in hushed tones, "I want spread happiness." Sonia looked back, gripping a green yoga mat. "Andrea, lets open a yoga studio," she replied. They looked at each other with joyful feelings, like two strange, young swans singing at a very happy birthday, which had soothing music playing in the background and two girls on the beach. Sonia grinned "lets do it".

-Drea & Soni
